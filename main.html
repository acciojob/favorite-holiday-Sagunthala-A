My favorite holiday destination is Bali. It is a beautiful island in Indonesia, known for its beaches, rice terraces, and temples.

  1. Ubud Monkey Forest: A nature reserve and Hindu temple complex in Ubud, Bali.
  2. Kuta Beach: One of Bali's most famous beaches, known for its great surfing waves.
  3. Tegalalang Rice Terrace: A UNESCO World Heritage site, famous for its beautiful rice terraces and traditional irrigation system.
  4. Pura Tanah Lot: A beautiful temple situated on a rock formation in the sea, known for its stunning sunsets.
  5. Uluwatu Temple: A beautiful temple situated on a cliff with breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean.
  • Nasi Goreng: A traditional Indonesian fried rice dish.
  • Sate: Grilled meat skewers served with peanut sauce.
  • Babi Guling: Roast pig served with rice, vegetables, and spicy sauce.
  • Nasi Campur: A mixed rice dish served with vegetables, meat, and sometimes seafood.
  • Gado-Gado:i> A salad made with mixed vegetables, tofu, tempeh, and peanut sauce.
